Budapest City Apartment

Why not combine your stay in Irota’s pristine natural beauty with a bit of big city hustle and bustle? Budapest is well on its way to becoming one of Europe’s prime destinations and it is easy to understand why: its stunning architectural beauty, its rich cultural life, its excellent bars and restaurants… well, you get the point.

Irota EcoLodge is proud to offer you a great package deal, which enables you to stay a few days in our wonderful apartment in Budapest before hitting the countryside – or the other way around: after all the relaxation in Irota, you submerge in the temptations of the glorious Hungarian capital.

Our modern and cosy apartment is located in on of the loveliest, leafiest streets of the Diplomats’ Quarter (Diplomátanegyed), just 3 minutes from Heroes’ Square (Hősök tere) and 5 min walk from the famous Széchenyi spa and the City Park (Városliget). It has 2 bedrooms plus a lovely living room, free Wifi and welcomes up to 6 guests. Of course it is fully equipped and comfortably furnished. The underground and trolley bus stops are only a 2 minutes walk from the apartment, while free parking space is just around the corner.

You can easily add the city option to your Irota EcoLodge booking. Of course we gladly provide you with more information if needed.
